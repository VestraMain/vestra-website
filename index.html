Vibrant Bond — Community Engagement Strategy & Implementation

Vestra is leading the community engagement strategy for Denver's Vibrant Bond initiative — a landmark investment in Denver's parks, culture, and public infrastructure. Our role is to ensure that residents from every neighborhood, background, and language community have a meaningful voice in how these funds are used. We develop multilingual outreach strategies, facilitate community listening sessions, and build coalitions with trusted community organizations to maximize participation from historically underrepresented communities.

Denver Broncos New Stadium — Community Engagement

Vestra has been engaged to develop and lead the community engagement strategy for the new Denver Broncos stadium project. We are working to ensure that surrounding neighborhoods and diverse community stakeholders are meaningfully included in the planning process — particularly communities of color and immigrant populations most directly impacted by the development.

How We Work

Our Approach

01
Listen First
We begin every engagement by listening — to community members, organizational partners, and existing data. Understanding the landscape before designing any strategy is non-negotiable.
02
Meet People Where They Are
Both geographically and culturally. We combine digital engagement, in-person community presence, and deep partnerships with trusted local organizations to expand participation.
03
Design for Equity
Every strategy we design prioritizes historically underrepresented communities. We don't just invite people to the table — we ensure the table is accessible, welcoming, and built for them.
04
Build for the Long Term
We don't do drive-by engagement. Our work is designed to build lasting relationships and sustainable community trust that extends well beyond any single project or initiative.
